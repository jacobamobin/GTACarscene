Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 19, 2024

Introduction

Welcome to Ontario Car Meets. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect and use your information when you use our application.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Device Information: We collect your device's UUID (Universally Unique Identifier) to authenticate your device and enable posting functionality. This is the only personal identifier we collect from regular users.
  • Meet Information: When you create a meet, we collect:
    • Meet title and description
    • Host information
    • Location data (address and coordinates)
    • Start and end times
    • Instagram URLs (if provided)
    • Photo URL (if provided)
  • Usage Analytics: We collect anonymous usage data to understand:
    • Which meets are most popular
    • General app usage patterns
    • Performance metrics

How We Use Your Information/Location

We use your information/location for the following purposes:

  • To authenticate your device and enable posting functionality
  • To display meets on our map interface
  • To improve our service based on usage patterns
  • To ensure the security and proper functioning of our application
